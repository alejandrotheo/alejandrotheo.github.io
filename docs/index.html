About Me

Who I Am

I'm Alejandro Theodosio Esmorís, an architecture student from A Coruña, Spain, currently in my 5th year at the University of A Coruña. My journey in architecture is driven by a passion for creating spaces that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also highly functional and efficient.

I believe in the power of design to improve people's lives and the importance of sustainable practices in modern architecture. My approach combines technical skills with creative thinking to develop innovative solutions.

When I'm not studying or working on projects, you can find me exploring new architectural styles, sketching urban landscapes, or enjoying the beautiful Galician coastline that inspires much of my work.

My Motivation

What motivates me most is the challenge of balancing form and function. I'm particularly interested in how architecture can respond to environmental concerns while meeting human needs.

Future Goals:

I aspire to contribute to architectural projects that make a positive impact on communities while respecting environmental constraints. My goal is to develop expertise in sustainable urban development and innovative housing solutions.
